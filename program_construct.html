How Does the Program Work?
  1. You become a part of the “Apollo AVC Program” basis your past performance and RAS contribution and a tier is allotted to you by Apollo.
  2. To make most of the AVC Program, you need to be complete your profile which can be done through My Account section.
  3. Your registration is authorized with a single mobile number which will be your unique identification for the program.
  4. Points will be accumulated on the basis of the segment and slabs as per the T&C of the program.
  5. The total points accumulated in Apollo AVC program can be used against redeeming the rewards.
  6. The available rewards in the catalogue can be redeemed under the Apollo AVC Program.
  7. Benefits are applicable for all the tiers and remember higher the tier greater the benefits so, always attempt to upgrade.
  8. The levels can be upgraded or degraded on the basis of the RAS.
  9. PBC Elite which is a top tier with exclusive benefits is not automatic but only through company’s invitation and HO approval.
